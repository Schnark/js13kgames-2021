Space Exploration

Explore space by flying from planet to planet.

To leave the orbit of a planet just click or tap the screen, or press the spacebar.

The more successful you are, the more coins you get. Use these coins to unlock new spaceships for your next explorations.

Spaceships

Select your spaceship to start the game.

Collect coins to unlock new spaceships. Currently, you have coins.

The last spaceship is exclusively for Coil subscribers.

Planets

There is nothing special about blue planets.
Orange planets are cloudy, which makes it hard to see where you are.
Red planets are dangerous! Leave them as soon as possible after arrival.
If you reach a golden planet you get 10 coins.
If you reach a silver planet you get 5 coins.
Green planets will brighten up when it is a good time to leave.
If you get lost in space after leaving a violet planet, a wormhole will appear and save you once.
