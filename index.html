Space Exploration

Click/tap/press spacebar to leave orbit, aim for planets, try to go as far as possible.

Spaceships

Select your spaceship to start the game.

Planets

There is nothing special about blue planets.
Orange planets are cloudy, which makes it hard to see where you are.
Red planets are dangerous! Leave them as soon as possible after arrival.
If you reach a golden planet you get 100 coins.
If you reach a silver planet you get 50 coins.
